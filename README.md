I am an independent builder in Japan, exploring how software and AI can turn complex work into systems people can understand, trust, and use every day.

### What I build

- **Automation systems** that make repeated work observable and reproducible
- **Decision-support tools** that clarify information without replacing human judgment
- **AI orchestration** with explicit roles, boundaries, and review points

### Working principles

```text
Read first. Make small changes. Keep decisions visible.
Prefer reversible systems. Leave judgment with the human.
```

Most of the larger systems are developed privately. Public repositories here are small, focused tools released as they become useful beyond their original context.
